iCare is dedicated to providing a platform for parents to mitigate the pressure from childcare, give them more free time and solve the problem that mothers are expected to sacrifice for the family.

We have always been believing that everybody, especially a mother, has the right to pursue her passion, whether in her career or her lifestyle. She should never bear the sole responsibility of providing childcare and thus, sacrifice her desire in her career.

Therefore, we constructs this platform for both parents and childcare providers to spread their information, request and provide babysitting services, so as to reduce the incidents that women sacrifice their careers for their babies.
